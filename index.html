Why Mahmutlar?

Mahmutlar, a seaside-town in Turkey

Mahmutlar is a neighbourhood of the municipality and district of Alanya, Antalya Province, Turkey. Its population is 53,034. The town has been transformed by the building of holiday homes and apartments for European tourists. Roads and services have been improved to cater for the growing tourist industry.

The beach line stretches along the entire district. The district has both pebble and poured-sandy beaches. The main attractions are the river Dimchay with bays for growing trout, and a cave of the same name located nearby, which is covered with stalactites and stalagmites. The district is young and actively developing.
